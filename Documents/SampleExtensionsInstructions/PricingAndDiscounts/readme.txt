The samples show how to customize pricing engine.

1. NewDiscountSamples.docx shares the main picture of pricing engine and retail discount extensibility story. In addition, it explains two new discount types: amount cap and discount offer with line filter.
2. PricingSampleTests.docx talks about black box component testing for pricing engine.
3. ContosoUpdate.sql: sample channel side schema for amount cap.
